Depth of Knowledge

Specialist Expertise

DPDPA 2023 & DPDP Rules 2025
Granular command of all provisions, phased commencement structure, penalty schedule, SDF obligations, and the s.7 legitimate uses framework. Tracking Rules development and DPAB guidance from formation.
GDPR & UK GDPR / DUAA 2025
Practical GDPR compliance including ROPA, DPIAs, SCCs, Transfer Impact Assessments, and cross-border transfer structures. DUAA 2025 changes including Recognised LI, SAR stop-the-clock, and modified ADM framework.
Multi-Jurisdictional Divergence Analysis
Identifying where frameworks conflict or require separate compliance tracks — including DPDPA vs GDPR on legitimate interests, children's data age thresholds, breach notification scope, and cross-border transfer models.
Data Processing Agreements
Drafting and reviewing DPAs under DPDPA s.8(2), GDPR Art. 28, LGPD Art. 37, Thailand PDPA Sec. 40, and analogous provisions. Aligning processor contracts to multi-framework obligations.
Breach Response & Incident Management
Advising on multi-framework breach notification obligations across 8 jurisdictions with different authority timelines, individual notification thresholds, and regulator temperaments.
