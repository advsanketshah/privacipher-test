Legal Accuracy Corrections
Saudi PDPL: Legitimate Interests Basis Added (2023 Amendment)
Updated to reflect Royal Decree M/148 (27 March 2023) which introduced legitimate interests as a valid lawful basis for non-sensitive personal data.
Saudi PDPL | Royal Decree M/148, 27 March 2023
Legal Correction
DPDPA Rule 7: Breach Notification is a Two-Step Regime
Corrected breach logic to reflect the final DPDP Rules 2025: immediate notification to both DPAB and affected individuals, followed by a detailed 72-hour report.
DPDP Rules 2025 Rule 7(1), 7(2)(a), 7(2)(b)
Legal Correction
Brazil LGPD Breach Notification: 3 Business Days
Corrected deadline to 3 business days under ANPD Resolution No. 15/2024, removing the incorrect 2-day reference from earlier resolution drafts.
LGPD Art. 48 | ANPD Resolution CD/ANPD No. 15/2024
