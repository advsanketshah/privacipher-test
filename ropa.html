Why do I need this? A Record of Processing Activities (RoPA) is a mandatory requirement under GDPR Article 30 for most organisations, and serves as the foundational document for compliance under the DPDPA, CCPA, and other global frameworks. It demonstrates accountability and is typically the first document requested by regulators during an audit.
