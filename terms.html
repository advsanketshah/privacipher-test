01

About This Tool

Privacipher is a free, browser-based self-assessment tool developed by Adv. Sanket Shah, an advocate practising in AI & Tech Law, data protection, and contract lifecycle management.

It generates a compliance score, risk heat map, penalty exposure estimate, and remediation roadmap based on the responses you enter. All processing happens in your browser. Nothing is transmitted to any server.

02

Not Legal Advice

This Tool does not constitute legal advice. Nothing in this Tool is legal advice. Using this Tool does not create an advocate-client relationship.

The Tool is an educational and informational resource. It is not a substitute for professional legal advice tailored to your specific circumstances. Before making compliance decisions, you should consult a qualified lawyer.

03

Accuracy & Limitations

Reasonable efforts have been made to ensure the accuracy of legal references and penalty figures. However, the regulatory landscape evolves rapidly. Statutory text referenced is for informational purposes only. The Tool cannot verify your inputs; inaccurate responses will produce inaccurate outputs.
